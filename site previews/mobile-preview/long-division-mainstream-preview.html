Long Division Mainstream

Non-scaffolded mobile preview page with written steps only.

Steps

  1. Divide: Look at the first number(s) that can be divided.
  2. Multiply: Put the answer on top, then multiply by the divisor.
  3. Subtract: Subtract to find what is left.
  4. Bring down: Bring down the next digit and repeat.
  5. Check remainder: Stop when no digits are left to bring down.
