# Intervention Station Marketing Master Plan

Last updated: 2026-03-21

## Core rule

Lead with the generators.

Everything else should make the membership feel deeper, stronger, and more complete, but the generators are the main reason a teacher stops scrolling and thinks:

"Wait. This makes the practice for me."

## Best high-level offer

Intervention Station gives teachers one membership where they can:

- generate fresh reading and math practice in seconds
- open scaffolded support resources by skill
- use games and songs to reinforce the same skill
- stop hunting across multiple websites

## Best simple value pitch

For `$9.99/month`, teachers are not buying one worksheet.

They are buying:

- a bank of generators that keeps making new practice
- a library of scaffolded teaching resources
- more than one way to teach the same skill

## Best audience framing for your following

You have a large audience, so your videos should not feel like standard ads.

They should feel like:

- teaching shortcuts
- teacher hacks
- "watch me make tomorrow's practice in 10 seconds"
- "here's what I wish teachers had for small groups"
- "I built this because teachers keep needing more repetition"

Your audience is much more likely to respond to demonstrated usefulness than to polished generic sales language.

## Best creator angles for videos

### 1. Time-saving angle

Promise:

- make tomorrow's practice fast
- stop searching for worksheets

Why it works:

- broadest pain point
- easiest to understand instantly

### 2. Small-group angle

Promise:

- this makes intervention planning easier
- create different pages for different groups

Why it works:

- especially good for RTI, special ed, interventionists, and classroom teachers

### 3. Repetition angle

Promise:

- some kids need way more repetition than one worksheet
- now you can generate more without extra work

Why it works:

- emotionally true
- directly tied to real classroom pain

### 4. "One skill, many ways" angle

Promise:

- make the worksheet
- grab the printable
- use the song
- open the game

Why it works:

- shows the membership is not shallow

### 5. Price angle

Promise:

- this is only `$9.99/month`
- if it saves one hour, it pays for itself

Why it works:

- strong conversion closer
- should usually come after value is clear
